# Design System Document

## Theme Overview
This document outlines the core design principles and token values for our digital products.

## Color Palette
Our color palette is designed to be vibrant and accessible.
- **Primary Color:** `#EB5E55` - Used for primary actions, key calls to action, and branding.
- **Neutral Color:** `#F8F9FA` - Provides a clean and light background for most UI elements.
- **Color Mode:** Light

## Typography
We maintain a consistent typographic hierarchy for readability and brand recognition.
- **Headline Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Label Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans

## Shape and Spacing
Our design language emphasizes clarity and a refined aesthetic.
- **Roundedness:** Moderate (2) - Corners have a subtle curve, providing a friendly yet professional feel.
- **Spacing:** Normal (2) - Elements are spaced for good readability and a balanced layout, avoiding a overly dense or sparse appearance.
